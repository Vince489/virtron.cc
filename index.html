Esports Athletic Commission

The Esports Athletic Commission (EAC) is Virtron's official regulatory body dedicated to overseeing and regulating various esports, with a primary focus on combat sports such as boxing and mixed martial arts within the metaverse.

The commission plays a pivotal role in licensing, supervising stakeholders, and ensuring the fair and ethical conduct of unarmed combat esports.
